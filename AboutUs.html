The Bertha’s Deluxe Chocolates factory founded in Sandiego, Califorina by the Bertha.
Bertha is a fan of chocolates and used to mix different types of Chocolates and tried to make her own recipe.
Eventually she developed a passion of making chocolates and her recipes had become famous in the local market and their chocolates became famous.
After Bertha,there family take over the business while continuing original recipes they expanded business operations all over the world with their quality and authentic recipes of the products.
Today Bertha’s chocolates are famous all over the world with their stores operating over 100 countries.
